--- The Open Source Manifesto of codespace ---
Date: 30 March 2026

I believe that software should be built in the open. Every day, I rely on tools like VS Code to do my work and expand my skills.
To me, the core of the open-source philosophy comes down to one word: Choice.
If I could contribute to this ecosystem, I would build a personal water purifier and share its source code freely so that others can learn, modify, and improve it.
